Rainbow Colored Fruit Smoothie

24 December, 2023 190
  • Twitter
  • Facebook

Ingredients:

    Red Layer (Strawberry):

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Orange Layer (Mango):

  • 1 cup chopped mango
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Yellow Layer (Pineapple):

  • 1 cup chopped pineapple
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Green Layer (Spinach & Kiwi):

  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 2 kiwis, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Blue Layer (Blueberry):

  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Purple Layer (Blackberry):

  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener (optional)

    • Instructions:

    Blend each fruit layer separately with the corresponding ingredients until smooth.

    You can adjust sweetness by adding honey or any preferred sweetener.

    Start layering the smoothie in a glass or jar, beginning with the red strawberry layer at the bottom, followed by the mango, pineapple, spinach & kiwi, blueberry, and blackberry layers.

    Pour each layer slowly and gently over the previous one for a distinct layered effect.

    Once all layers are added, you can garnish the top with a slice of fruit or a colorful straw.
